# TERM SHEET — STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

**CONFIDENTIAL — FOR DISCUSSION PURPOSES ONLY**

## Parties
- **Buyer:** Apex Capital Partners Fund VII, L.P. ("Buyer")
- **Seller:** DataFlow Analytics, Inc. shareholders (collectively, "Sellers")
- **Target:** DataFlow Analytics, Inc. ("Company")

## Transaction Structure
- **Type:** Stock Purchase Agreement
- **Purchase Price:** $150,000,000 (One Hundred Fifty Million Dollars)
- **Consideration:** 100% cash at closing, subject to customary adjustments
- **Working Capital Target:** $8,500,000 (trailing 12-month average)
- **Working Capital Collar:** +/- $500,000

## Purchase Price Adjustments
- Cash / cash equivalents: Added to Purchase Price
- Indebtedness: Deducted from Purchase Price
- Transaction expenses: Deducted from Purchase Price
- Working capital adjustment: True-up within 90 days post-closing

## Escrow
- **General Indemnity Escrow:** $15,000,000 (10% of Purchase Price)
  - Release: 50% at 12 months, 50% at 18 months post-closing
- **Special Indemnity Escrow:** $7,500,000 (5% of Purchase Price)
  - For tax and employee classification matters
  - Release: 24 months post-closing

## Representations and Warranties
- **Seller Fundamental Reps:** Organization, authority, capitalization, no conflicts
  - Survival: Until applicable statute of limitations
- **General Reps:** Financial statements, material contracts, IP, employees, taxes, environmental, litigation
  - Survival: 18 months post-closing
- **Special Reps:** Tax, employee benefits, data privacy
  - Survival: 36 months post-closing
- **Buyer Reps:** Authority, financing, no conflicts
  - Survival: 18 months post-closing

## Indemnification
- **Cap:** $15,000,000 (10% of Purchase Price) for general reps
- **Basket:** $750,000 (0.5% of Purchase Price), tipping basket
- **Mini-basket:** $25,000 per claim
- **Fundamental Reps Cap:** Purchase Price
- **R&W Insurance:** Buyer to obtain, $30M coverage, retention $750,000

## Key Covenants
- Non-compete: Sellers for 3 years, 50-mile radius
- Non-solicitation: Sellers for 2 years (employees and customers)
- Transition services: Up to 12 months post-closing
- Key employee retention: CEO and CTO for minimum 24 months

## Conditions to Closing
- No Material Adverse Effect
- HSR clearance (if required)
- Third-party consents (key contracts)
- Key employee agreements executed
- R&W insurance bound
- Minimum cash of $2,000,000 at closing

## Closing
- **Target Closing Date:** 60 days from execution of definitive agreement
- **Outside Date:** 120 days from execution
- **Governing Law:** Delaware

## Exclusivity
- 45-day exclusivity period from execution of this term sheet

## Expenses
- Each party bears its own expenses
- Buyer responsible for HSR filing fees
- Seller responsible for tail D&O insurance

## Non-Binding
This term sheet is non-binding except for exclusivity, confidentiality, and expenses provisions.
